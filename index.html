Who We Are

DeValk's Greenhouse and Gardens is a local Green Bay greenhouse offering a variety of annuals including hanging baskets and flower pouches of wave petunias, million bells, moss roses, etc. Window boxes, large flower planters, and garden dish arrangements, accent plants, spikes, vines, ivy, bedding plants, succulents and a variety of vegetable plants and herbs.

Don't have a lot of time to plant your flower arrangements? Not sure what combination of plants will do best throughout the summer? Owner, Brenda DeValk, would love to help you pick out the right combination of annuals for your yard.

COVID-19 Update

In light of the COVID-19 pandemic, we are taking extra measures to keep our customers healthy and safe. We ask that anyone visiting the greenhouse bring with them only the essential people needed to shop. Children are allowed only if they stay by their caregivers at all times. Feel free to wear a cloth face covering in the greenhouse if you have one. We are sanitizing high traffic surfaces frequently. We have a designated path to follow through the greenhouses to ensure social distancing. We understand these requests are above and beyond our usual business operations. We want to be able to serve all our customers throughout the season while keeping our customers and ourselves healthy and well.
