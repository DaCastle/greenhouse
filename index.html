Who We Are

Devalk's Greenhouse and Gardens is a locally grown greenhouse offering a variety of annuals including hanging baskets and flower pouches, of wave petunias, million bells, moss roses, etc. Window box arrangements, large flower planter arrangements, garden dish arrangements, accent plants, spikes, vines, ivy, bedding plants and a variety of vegetable plants and herbs.

We sell unique, locally-made lawn ornaments along with wind chimes and solar accent lights for hanging baskets and pots. We also have a selection of vegetable seeds available, as well as, potting soil.

Don't have a lot of time to plant your flower arrangements? Not sure what combination of plants will do best throughout the summer? Owner, Brenda DeValk, would love to help you pick out the right combination of annuals for your yard. You can even bring your pots to the greenhouse and she will plant your flower arrangements for you.
